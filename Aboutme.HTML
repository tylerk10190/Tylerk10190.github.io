About Me

Tyler Koepke was born and raised in Miwalaukee and still lives here with his wife and two daughters. He has a heart for the city of Milwaukee and he wants to do something that works to make it a better place for all the people who live here. He spent four years in the Marine Corps infantry where he served on two deployments overseas; one to Afghanistan and one to Okinawa, Japan. He attended Marquette University where he studied Sociology and Philosophy and graduated with Honors. His interests include spending time with his family, watching sports and movies, and playing video games.
